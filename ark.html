About Ark:survival evolved

Ark is a survivalgame where you first spawn on an island with nothing but unerwere.
You will quickly find out that you are not the only one thats on that island but there is also
extinct animals such as dinosaurs. If you by some DLC you will for example spawn in a desert.

Arkis playable on many platforms here are all the platforms: Steam(PC), xboxs, playstation, mobile and switch
